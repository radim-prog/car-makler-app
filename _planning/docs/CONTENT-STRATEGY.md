## 📊 CONTENT STRATEGY OVERVIEW

### 3-Pillar Content Architecture

```
TOFU (Top of Funnel) - AWARENESS
├── "Jak prodat auto" guides
├── Market insights
└── Industry news

MOFU (Middle of Funnel) - CONSIDERATION
├── "Automobilový makléř vs. X" comparisons
├── Process explainers
└── ROI calculators

BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) - DECISION
├── Case studies
├── Client testimonials
└── FAQ & objection handling
```

### Content Types & Frequency

| Content Type | Frequency | Purpose | Channel |
|--------------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Blog Articles | 2x/týden | SEO, Authority | Web |
| Case Studies | 1x/měsíc | Social Proof | Web, Email |
| Video Content | 1x/týden | Engagement | YouTube, IG |
| Email Newsletter | 1x/týden | Nurture | Email |
| Social Posts | 5x/týden | Brand Awareness | FB, IG, LinkedIn |
| Podcast (budoucnost) | 2x/měsíc | Thought Leadership | Spotify |

## 📝 CONTENT STYLE GUIDE

### Voice & Tone

**Voice** (consistent):
- Expert but approachable
- Confident but not arrogant
- Helpful, not pushy

**Tone** (varies by context):
- Blog: Educational, authoritative
- Social: Casual, engaging
- Email: Personal, direct
- Video: Energetic, authentic

### Writing Rules

1. **Short sentences.** (Max 20 words ideal)
2. **Active voice.** ("Prodáme auto" ne "Auto bude prodáno")
3. **Specific numbers.** (17 dní, not "rychle")
4. **"You" not "we"** focus
5. **Stories over stats** (but use both)

### Formatting

- Headings every 300 words
- Bullets for lists (easier to scan)
- Bold for emphasis (sparingly)
- Images every 500 words
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
